Pomegranates from Kandahar

Pomegranates from Kandahar are some of the sweetest and juiciest in Afghanistan. Known for their deep red color and rich flavor, these fruits are a symbol of Afghan agriculture. People around the world enjoy them in juices, salads, and desserts, making them an important part of Afghan culture and cuisine.
